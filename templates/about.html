About App Engine People

App Engine People lists Google App Engine developers from around the world, and aims to help developers find like-minded people near them and possibly start up some local meet-ups and user groups. The App Engine People website is not affiliated with Google Inc. and was built as a clone of DjangoPeople.net by the people that run devsar & livemade:.

For feedback, suggestions or to contact us, please email us at info@livemade.com
